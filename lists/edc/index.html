EDC

Every Day Carry.

It can mean so many things to different people.

I live in an urban environment, in a safe first world country, with strict laws regarding weapons, including knives.

Still, I entertain myself with the thought that carrying certain things may one save my day sometime.

That’s why I have a small list of items I carry with me most of the time.

Multi-tool

A good multi-tool is the most used item of all my EDC list, something I actually use somewhat regularly.

The pliers are indispesible when fixing a zipper, or pulling a screw from the tyre.

The scissors are a must for dealing with a hangnail on a finger, or lose threads on my clothes.

Screwdriver can be useful when dealing with small ad-hoc repairs at random places, like rental properties.

It is illegal to carry a knife in my country, unless you need it for the legitimate reason, like hiking or work.

I don’t mind this restriction, but obviously this heavily limits what multi-tool I can carry - it must not have a blade.

Thanfully, for my needs, scissors are much more useful.

Anyway, I landed on two multi-tools:

Gerber Multi-Plier 600 Bladeless

I carry it in my backpack.

It is heavy, durable, has tons of tools, and can handle anything.

Way to bulky and heavy to carry in the pocket, but I love it.

Nextool Mini Sailor Lite

Small, lightweight, with good enough scissors. I carry it in my pocket, in a EDC pouch.

Can be used for some emergency repairs, but if I have a backpack - I’ll use the indestructible MP600.
