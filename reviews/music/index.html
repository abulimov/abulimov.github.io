Music Album Reviews

Here you can find my review scores for the music albums I extensively listened to.

Which almost always means that I bought them on Bandcamp.

I’m writing my mini-reviews since 2013, just for fun. The reviews are not published (yet?), but the scores are.

All scores are given on a scale from 0 to 10, with exceptional titles given 11 (because why not?).
